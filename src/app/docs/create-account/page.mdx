# Create an Account

Getting started with Foliocraft takes less than a minute. You can sign up using email and password, or instantly connect with GitHub or Google.

## Sign Up Options

### Email & Password

1. Go to the [registration page](/register)
2. Fill in the following fields:
   - **Full Name** — your display name (2–50 characters)
   - **Email** — a valid email address (must be unique)
   - **Password** — minimum 8 characters, must include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one number
   - **Confirm Password** — re-enter your password to verify
3. Click **Create Account**
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard immediately

### GitHub Login

1. On the [login page](/login) or [register page](/register), click the **GitHub** button
2. GitHub will ask you to authorize Foliocraft to access your public profile information (name, email, avatar)
3. Click **Authorize** on GitHub's page
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard automatically
5. Your GitHub profile picture becomes your Foliocraft avatar

### Google Login

1. Click the **Google** button on the login or register page
2. Select your Google account or enter your Google credentials
3. You'll be redirected to your dashboard automatically
4. Your Google profile picture becomes your Foliocraft avatar

> **Linking accounts:** If you sign up with email and later try to log in with GitHub/Google using the same email, the accounts are automatically linked. You can then use either method to log in.

## After Sign Up

Once your account is created, you'll land on the **Dashboard**. From here you can:

- **Create your first portfolio** — click the + New Portfolio button in the sidebar
- **Update your profile** — go to Settings to change your name, username, bio, and profile picture
- **Set your preferences** — choose light or dark mode, configure notifications

## Your Username

Foliocraft automatically generates a username from your email address. For example, `john@example.com` becomes `john`. You can change it anytime in **Settings > Profile > Username**.

Your username determines your public portfolio URL:

`yourdomain.com/portfolio/your-username`

### Username Rules
- Minimum 3 characters, maximum 30 characters
- Only lowercase letters, numbers, hyphens (`-`), and underscores (`_`)
- Must be unique — no two users can have the same username

## Password Requirements

If using email/password authentication:

- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter (A–Z)
- At least one lowercase letter (a–z)
- At least one number (0–9)

You can change your password later in **Settings > Account > Change Password**.

> **Tip:** If you sign up with GitHub/Google and later want to set a password for email login, you can do so from the Settings page.
